Week 1:

Gestures

Gesture In Soccer

  • Long Kick: When the striker wants to tell the goalkeeper to kick the ball as far as possible.
  • Switch: Fingers across, when left and right wingers want to switch positions for better attacking opportunities.
  • Slide Tackle: One hand slides on the other, indicating “slide tackle”.

More Soccer Gestures

  • In My Blood: When scoring a stunning goal, you touch your blood vessels, saying “It’s in my blood!!!”
  • On Target: When scoring an accurate goal right in the corner!
  • It’s a Trap: In soccer, defenders may set an “offset” trap, making the opponent’s offense invalid.

HTML Practice

Using Paper Cuts for HTML Practice

We used papercuts as HTML tags to imitate the foundational structure of the YouTube homepage:

  • Header Section (<div> with <h1> and <input>)
    • The YouTube logo (<h1>) is positioned at the top, establishing brand identity.
    • A search bar (<input>) allows users to quickly find videos, mimicking YouTube's core navigation feature.
  • Main Content Area (<section> with <div>)
    • This section contains multiple video thumbnails wrapped in <a> tags, making them clickable links.
    • Each thumbnail (<img>) is accompanied by a title (<p>), mirroring YouTube's video grid format.
  • Navigation & Interactive Elements
    • Various <div> elements simulate YouTube’s structured categories.
    • The clickable images inside <a> tags represent video previews, ensuring easy access to content.
